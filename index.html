How did you end up in the nightmare house?

You were trick-or-treating when the door to the house blew open mysteriously and a voice called you in. You assumed for the candy. Turns out: only nightmares.

You've always felt uneasy about the house, but the resident cool kid said you might be cool enough for the cool party if you spent the night in the nightmare house.

With the cost of living rapidly on the rise, you couldn't turn down the incredible deal you got from a old man you've never seen before who gave you the house for free on condition that you signed the contract in a teensy bit of blood.

You're just a misunderstood monster who thought the gig haunting the nightmare house sounded fun at the time, but now you're a slave to the grind and you're ready to follow your real passion: photography.
